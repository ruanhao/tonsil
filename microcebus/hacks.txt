## increase the indentation
>G # indent to right for current line and afterwards
>> # indent current line to right

## delete a word
daw

## add or subtract
{NUMBER}<C-a> # add {NUMBER} to the number under the cursor
{NUMBER}<C-x> # subtract {NUMBER} to the number under the cursor

## swap case
guaw # lowercase current word
gUaw # uppercase current word
gUU  # uppercase current line
guu  # lowercase current line

## output from Register (Insert Mode)
<C-r>{REGISTER}      # output the content of Register {REGISTER}
<C-r><C-p>{REGISTER} # output the content of Register {REGISTER} without unintended indentation
